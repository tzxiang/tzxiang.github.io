About

Tian-Zhu Xiang is working as a researcher and Technical Manager of Applied AI at Space42. Before that, he was a researcher at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI). He received his PhD from LIESMARS, Wuhan University in 2019. His research mainly focuses on computer vision, remote sensing, and medical image analysis. Specifically, he is interested in camouflaged scene understanding, visual saliency, medical image segmentation, and remote sensing image analysis.
