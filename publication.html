<< Tian-Zhu Xiang   向天烛

Tian-Zhu Xiang is working as a Technical Lead in GeoAI at G42 Bayanat. Previously, he was a researcher at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence working with Dr. Fan Zhu and Prof. Ling Shao. He received his PhD from LIESMARS, Wuhan University in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Gui-Song Xia and Prof. Liangpei Zhang.
