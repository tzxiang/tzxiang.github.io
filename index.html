About

Currently I am a research associate in Inception Institute of Artifical Intelligence (IIAI), UAE. Before that, I got my Ph.D. degree in LIESMARS at Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Gui-Song Xia & Prof. Liangpei Zhang. My research interests include image alignment and stitching, computational photography, object detection, etc.

Educations

  • M.Sc in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing, 2012-2015, Wuhan University, China
