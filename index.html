Tian-Zhu Xiang   向天烛

Tian-Zhu Xiang is working as a Tech Lead in GeoAI at G42 Bayanat. Before that, he was a researcher at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence working with Dr. Fan Zhu and Prof. Ling Shao. He received his PhD from LIESMARS, Wuhan University in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Gui-Song Xia and Prof. Liangpei Zhang.

Research Interests

  • Object Detection: Camouflaged Object Detection (COD), Rotation Object Detection (ROD)

  • Visual Saliency: RGB-D Salient Object Detection (RGB-D SOD), RGB-T SOD, RS SOD, CoSOD

  • Computational Photography: Image Alighment & Stitching, Image Fusion

  • Medical Image Processing: Cell Segmentation, Polyp Segmentation

  • Remote Sensing Scene Understanding: ROD, RS SOD, Image Fusion, Shadow Detection
